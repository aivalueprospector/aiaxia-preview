# AI Value Prospectors — Cross-Twin Email Templates

The same idea sells both products: every person gets their own AI twin, and the person guiding them — a parent, a teacher, a business owner — gets a twin too. The two twins talk to each other, so you always know how things are going and you can step in without nagging or chasing. Pick the version that fits your recipient, fill in the tokens, and aim it at a family, a school, or a business.

## Tips

- Replace every token before sending — `{{Prospect Name}}`, `{{Company}}`, `{{Industry}}`, `{{Pain Point}}`, `{{Referrer}}`, `{{Demo Date}}`, `{{Demo Link}}`, `{{Rep Name}}`, `{{Rep Email}}` — and double-check that no `{{...}}` slips through to the inbox.
- For warm intros, name the real referrer and a real, specific pain point in their own words; a vague `{{Pain Point}}` reads like a mail merge.
- Swap the example phrases ("how's Emma doing in reading?", "where do the Q3 installs stand?") for something true to the recipient's world — a real subject, a real project, a real name on their team.
- Pick the version by recipient, not by length: aim Versions A–C at families, tutors, and schools; aim the business versions at owner-operators and the small teams around them.
