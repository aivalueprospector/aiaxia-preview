## Quick ROI Math

**Time Saved Searching:**
- 30 min/day × 5 days × 50 weeks = 125 hours/year/person
- At $50/hour = **$6,250 saved per employee per year**

**Report Automation:**
- 3-hour weekly report × 50 weeks = 150 hours/year
- Automated = **$7,500 saved per report type**

**Knowledge Retention:**
- Cost to replace senior employee knowledge: **$50,000-$200,000**

## Demo Flow (15 minutes)

1. **Chat** (3 min) - Ask a business question, show sources
2. **DATA** (3 min) - Show knowledge graph connections
3. **TASKS** (3 min) - Demonstrate automation template
4. **DOCS** (3 min) - Upload and process a document
5. **Close** (3 min) - Summarize value, discuss tier fit
