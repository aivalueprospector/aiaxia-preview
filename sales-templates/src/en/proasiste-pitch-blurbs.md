## Tips

- Replace every token before sending — `{{Prospect Name}}`, `{{Company}}`, `{{Industry}}`, `{{Pain Point}}`, `{{Referrer}}`, `{{Demo Link}}`, `{{Rep Name}}`, `{{Rep Email}}` — and double-check that no `{{...}}` slips through to the inbox.
- For warm intros, name the real referrer and a real, specific pain point in their own words; a vague `{{Pain Point}}` reads like a mail merge.
- Swap the example phrase ("where do the Q3 installs stand?") for a real project and a real name on their team.
- Aim Version A at owners cold, Version B at warm intros, and Version C when knowledge loss / key-person risk is the live worry.
