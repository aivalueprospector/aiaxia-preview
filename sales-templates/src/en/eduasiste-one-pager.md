## For parents, tutors & small schools

Every learner has their own AI twin. Parents and teachers have twins too. The twins talk to each other, so you always know how your child is growing, and you can guide them without nagging.

## Five things you can do the day you sign up

**1. See what your child learned today.** Ask your twin *"How's Emma doing in reading this week?"* and get a live answer pulled straight from her learning twin, without waiting for a report card. *(For parents who feel disconnected from school progress.)*

**2. Assign homework in 30 seconds.** Type *"practice times tables, 10 questions, due Friday"* and the system builds the worksheet for your child, plus a private answer key for you. *(For tutors, homeschoolers, supplemental-learning families.)*

**3. Study sessions that get summarized for you.** A good tutor asks questions and walks the child toward the answer instead of handing it over, and your child's twin works the same way. When the session ends, you get a summary in your own chat of what clicked and what didn't. *(For parents who want quality learning time without being the tutor.)*

**4. Goals that actually move.** Set a goal like *"read 20 books this semester."* Every time your child's twin records progress, the bar fills in on its own, so you don't have to chase manual check-ins. *(For achievement-oriented families and tutors running structured programs.)*

**5. Full transparency, built in.** Every time you look at your child's data, they can see it happened. There's no hidden monitoring, so your child always knows where they stand with you. *(For privacy-conscious parents, teen parents, and ethics-forward schools.)*
