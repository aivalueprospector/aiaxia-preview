## For parents, tutors & small schools

Every learner has their own AI twin. Parents and teachers have twins too. The twins talk to each other — so you always know how your child is growing, and you can guide them without nagging.

## Five things you can do the day you sign up

**1. See what your child learned today** — Ask your twin *"How's Emma doing in reading this week?"* — and get a live answer pulled straight from her learning twin. No report cards. No waiting. *(For parents who feel disconnected from school progress.)*

**2. Assign homework in 30 seconds** — Type *"practice times tables, 10 questions, due Friday"* — and the system builds the worksheet for your child and a private answer key for you. *(For tutors, homeschoolers, supplemental-learning families.)*

**3. Socratic study sessions, auto-summarized** — Your child's twin guides them through a topic by asking questions, not giving answers. When they're done, you get a summary of what clicked and what didn't — in your own chat. *(For parents who want quality learning time without being the tutor.)*

**4. Goals that actually move** — Set a goal like *"read 20 books this semester"* — and every time your child's twin records progress, the bar fills in automatically. No manual check-ins. *(For achievement-oriented families and tutors running structured programs.)*

**5. Full transparency, built in** — Every time you look at your child's data, they can see it happened. No secret surveillance. Trust is the product. *(For privacy-conscious parents, teen parents, and ethics-forward schools.)*
