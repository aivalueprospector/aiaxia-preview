## Five things you can do the day you sign up

**1. See what your team handled today** — Ask your twin *"Where do the Q3 installs stand?"* — and get a live answer pulled straight from your team's twins. No status meeting. No chasing updates. *(For owners who feel one step behind their own operation.)*

**2. Delegate in one sentence** — Type *"have Maria follow up on the Henderson quote by Friday"* — and the task lands in her twin's chat, tracked right back to yours. *(For owners who hold too much in their head and on sticky notes.)*

**3. Day-one context for new hires** — Every conversation, extracted fact, and filed document feeds a shared knowledge graph. New hires get years of context on day one — without having to ask anyone. *(For teams that lose institutional memory every time someone leaves.)*
