EduAsiste · Education · eduasiste.org

For parents, tutors & small schools

Every learner has their own AI twin. Parents and teachers have twins too. The twins talk to each other — so you always know how your child is growing, and you can guide them without nagging.

Five things you can do the day you sign up

1See what your child learned today

Ask your twin “How's Emma doing in reading this week?” — and get a live answer pulled straight from her learning twin. No report cards. No waiting.

For parents who feel disconnected from school progress.

2Assign homework in 30 seconds

Type “practice times tables, 10 questions, due Friday” — and the system builds the worksheet for your child and a private answer key for you.

For tutors, homeschoolers, supplemental-learning families.

3Socratic study sessions, auto-summarized

Your child's twin guides them through a topic by asking questions, not giving answers. When they're done, you get a summary of what clicked and what didn't — in your own chat.

For parents who want quality learning time without being the tutor.

4Goals that actually move

Set a goal like “read 20 books this semester” — and every time your child's twin records progress, the bar fills in automatically. No manual check-ins.

For achievement-oriented families and tutors running structured programs.

5Full transparency, built in

Every time you look at your child's data, they can see it happened. No secret surveillance. Trust is the product.

For privacy-conscious parents, teen parents, and ethics-forward schools.

Coming soon

Quizzes & tests with auto-grading · Classroom view for teachers managing cohorts · Co-parent collaboration on shared assignments.

Open with: “When's the last time you knew — in real time — exactly what your child was learning and where they were struggling?”

Then close: EduAsiste gives your child their own AI tutor — and gives you a parent twin that stays in the loop automatically. It's like having a teacher who reports to you after every lesson.

ProAsiste · Business · proasiste.com

For owner-operators & the small teams around them

Every person on your team has their own AI twin. You have an owner twin too. The twins talk to each other — so you always know what's getting handled, and you can steer the business without sitting in every meeting.

Five things you can do the day you sign up

1See what your team handled today

Ask your twin “Where do the Q3 installs stand?” — and get a live answer pulled straight from your team's twins. No status meeting. No chasing updates.

For owners who feel one step behind their own operation.

2Delegate in one sentence

Type “have Maria follow up on the Henderson quote by Friday” — and the task lands in her twin's chat, tracked right back to yours.

For owners who hold too much in their head and on sticky notes.

3Day-one context for new hires

Every conversation, extracted fact, and filed document feeds a shared knowledge graph. New hires get years of context on day one — without having to ask anyone.

For teams that lose institutional memory every time someone leaves.

4Goals the whole team moves

Set a target like “$75K recurring revenue by Q4” — and every team member's progress rolls up into the bar automatically. No spreadsheet wrangling.

For owners running structured quarters and accountable teams.

5Oversight with transparency

Administrator, employee, and mentor roles per seat — everyone sees who can see what. You get the full picture without black-box surveillance. Mentor seats are free.

For owners who want visibility their team actually trusts.
