One Twin Engine, Three Jobs

Pointed inward at your team, sideways at your industry, and outward at your customers.

ProAsiste, ExpertAsiste, and HelpAsiste are not three different products bolted together. They are the same twin engine doing three different jobs:

  • ProAsiste points the twin inward, at you and your team, to run the business.
  • ExpertAsiste points it sideways, at your industry, to sell what you know.
  • HelpAsiste points it outward, at your customers, to answer questions on your website.

The shared engine

Every twin runs on the same foundation, so a customer who learns one already understands the others.

  • One knowledge brain (KaiBrain). Documents, conversations, and spreadsheets become linked, searchable knowledge instead of a pile of files.
  • It learns while you work. No separate training step; normal activity feeds the brain.
  • Same way knowledge goes in. All three use the same upload-and-extract pipeline.
  • Transparency and portability built in. Access is visible; your knowledge stays yours.

The difference between the three is not the engine. It's how the engine is aimed.

ProAsiste · Run your own business Available

Inward, your team

Every person on your team has their own AI twin; you have an owner twin too. The twins talk to each other, so you always know what's getting handled and can steer the business without sitting in every meeting.

  • Two twins, one conversation. Your owner twin queries your team's twins. Ask “Where do the Q3 installs stand?” and answers come straight back.
  • A private brain with real names. A two-layer model: a private vault holding the full picture, and an obfuscated projection for the graph view. The owner sees the real thing; the structure is shared safely.
  • Roles and oversight. Administrator, employee, and mentor roles per seat, so everyone sees who can see what.

Direction: inward. Named, signed-in people. The twin remembers everything, because remembering is the product.

ExpertAsiste · Sell your expertise Emerging

Sideways, your industry

The same engine turned toward your professional peers and clients. A subject-matter expert, say a lawyer, an accountant, or an engineer, loads years of know-how into a twin, and others in the industry consult it. Think of Consuela, a Santiago lawyer with a 12-year practice, or Roberto, an accountant: each loads expertise once, and their twin answers the questions people would otherwise pay an hour just to ask.

  • Expertise becomes a corpus. The expert's knowledge is packaged as a shared corpus that subscribers' twins draw on, one corpus per subscription.
  • The expert curates; the industry consults. The same upload-and-extract pipeline ingests case notes, regulations, and playbooks.
  • A product you sell, not just a tool you use. ProAsiste makes you more effective; ExpertAsiste lets you monetize what you already know to a whole industry.

Direction: sideways. Peers and clients drawing on a corpus you own and rent out. In design: mockups exist, the platform is being built.

HelpAsiste · Answer your customers Forthcoming

Outward, your customers

The same engine pointed at the general public on your website. A visitor, maybe someone deciding whether to buy or someone stuck using what they bought, asks a question and gets an answer from your product knowledge. It is a distinct twin type, a public-facing variant of a ProAsiste business twin, built to be deliberately unlike ProAsiste where it matters.

  • A dedicated, PII-free brain. Its own fresh workspace, loaded only with product docs, FAQs, and manuals. It is never linked to your private ProAsiste brain, so nothing personal can leak.
  • It forgets on purpose. Visitor chats are stateless. They are held in memory for the session only and written nowhere. Only anonymized counts are kept.
  • Many strangers at once, no crossed wires. Each visitor gets an isolated session; sessions reset on idle timeout or when the visitor leaves.
  • A locked-down public key. The widget ships with a scoped, read-only embed token that can chat and nothing else. No sidebar, just the chat.
  • It doesn't eat your limits. Public traffic hits a separate twin, so it never burns your own daily usage.

Direction: outward. Anonymous strangers, a knowledge base with no secrets, and a twin that intentionally remembers nothing. Architected and assessed, not yet built.

How they fit together

ProAsiste is the hub. It's the twin the business owner lives in day to day.

HelpAsiste is a linked sibling. It hangs off the owner's ProAsiste twin but keeps its own clean, public knowledge base. It is connected for billing and management, walled off for privacy.

ExpertAsiste rides the knowledge layer. It takes the same brain and turns it into a corpus an expert can offer to a whole industry.

One engine, learned once, sold three ways.
