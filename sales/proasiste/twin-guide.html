What the ProAsiste twin does

A quick tour of the business twin: every person on your team gets their own AI twin, you get an owner twin, and the twins talk to each other so you can run the business without sitting in every meeting.

← ProAsiste materials

The shape of it

One owner twin + a twin per team member, all on the same knowledge brain. Documents, conversations, and spreadsheets become linked, searchable knowledge — and the engine learns while you work, with no separate training step. Below: roughly what the owner sees.

Features

Two twins, one conversation

Your owner twin queries your team's twins. Ask "Where do the Q3 installs stand?" and the answer comes straight back — no meeting required.

Knowledge graph

Documents, chats, and spreadsheets become linked, searchable entities, events, and relationships — instead of a pile of files nobody can find.

Private vault, safe projection

A two-layer model: a private vault holds the full picture with real names; an obfuscated projection drives the shared graph view. The owner sees the real thing; structure is shared safely.

Roles & oversight

Administrator, employee, and mentor roles per seat — so everyone can see who can see what, and nothing leaks across the team by accident.

Learns while you work

The same upload-and-extract pipeline turns everyday documents into knowledge automatically. No separate training step — normal activity feeds the brain.

Memory that sticks

Long-term recall of facts, preferences, and corrections — so the twin keeps getting more useful instead of forgetting between sessions.
