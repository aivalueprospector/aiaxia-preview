What the twin does

One owner twin + a twin per team member, all on the same knowledge brain. Documents, conversations, and spreadsheets become linked, searchable knowledge, and the engine learns while you work with no separate training step. Below: roughly what the owner sees.

Twins that work together

Your owner twin queries your team's twins. Ask "Where do the Q3 installs stand?" and the answer comes straight back — no meeting required.

Knowledge graph

Documents, chats, and spreadsheets become linked, searchable entities, events, and relationships, instead of a pile of files nobody can find.

Private vault, safe projection

A two-layer model: a private vault holds the full picture with real names; an obfuscated projection drives the shared graph view. The owner sees the real thing; structure is shared safely.

Roles & oversight

Administrator, employee, and mentor roles per seat, so everyone can see who can see what, and nothing leaks across the team by accident.

Learns while you work

The same upload-and-extract pipeline turns everyday documents into knowledge automatically. No separate training step. Normal activity feeds the brain.

Memory that sticks

Long-term recall of facts, preferences, and corrections, so the twin keeps getting more useful instead of forgetting between sessions.

Meet Mente

Mente — your professional assistant

Every ProAsiste twin speaks as Mente: professional, efficient, first-person. Mente leads with the answer and then the detail, asks one question at a time (never a compound question), and never uses emojis or em-dashes — output is built to drop straight into board decks, client emails, and legal filings.

Which twin you have

ProAsiste comes in three types. Which one you were set up with decides who can use it and what extra abilities you get.

Twin typeWho it's forWho uses it
Individual ProfessionalA solo business owner or single practitioner — no team.One administrator. Mente acts as your personal professional assistant.
Legal AdvisorA lawyer, partner, or legal counsel.One administrator, on a legal-specialized version of Mente (see Legal twins).
Professional OrganizationA multi-person firm or department.Administrators (org-wide view, cross-team patterns) and employees (their own + team-shared scope).
Industry tuning. Business twins can be tuned to a vertical (consulting, healthcare, finance, real estate, tech, manufacturing, logistics, and more). When a vertical match exists, Mente uses language and structure suited to that field; otherwise it uses the general business assistant.

The interface

You talk to Mente in the chat panel on the left. A sidebar on the right holds your tabs — and which tabs you see depends on your twin type and role. Here is the shape of it.

Your sidebar tabs

Notes is where you save chat messages to keep for reference. Uploads is where you add documents. Reports holds generated output. The set you see is decided by your twin type and role:

Twin & roleSidebar tabs
Individual Professional · administratorNotes · Uploads · Reports
Professional Organization · administrator or employeeNotes · Uploads · Reports
Legal Advisor · administratorNotes · Uploads (Reports hidden — see below)

Capture knowledge

The twin learns from your documents and conversations — there is no separate training step. Feeding it is the main thing you do on day one.

1

Open the Uploads tab

In the right sidebar, upload PDFs, Word documents, spreadsheets, or images. The twin extracts the text and structure automatically.

2

Let it build entity notes

From each document the twin creates entity notes — one per supplier, customer, employee, product, or process it finds — and links them together.

3

Your private vault stays private

The full picture, with real names, lives in a private markdown vault. A separate, obfuscated projection drives the shared graph view, so structure can be shown safely.

How your knowledge connects

Behind every answer is a knowledge graph. As you upload documents and talk to Mente, the twin pulls out the pieces and connects them automatically, with no separate training step and no graph screen to manage:

What it connectsExamples
EntitiesPeople, suppliers, customers, products, processes.
RelationshipsThe links between them — which supplier serves which customer, who owns which process.
Events & causesTimestamped activity — transactions, deadlines, incidents — and the causal links between them.
GoalsMeasurable objectives with progress tracking.

You explore it by asking. Mente walks the graph for you and answers in plain language, naming the entities and events behind each answer. See Ask your twin.

Ask your twin

The chat panel on the left is where most of the value is. Ask in plain language; Mente answers from your own knowledge and remembers across sessions.

✓

Ask anything about your business

"Which suppliers are behind this month?" · "Summarize the Riverside account." Mente answers from your entities and events, not the open internet.

✓

It remembers

Tell Mente a fact, preference, or correction once and it recalls it later. You can ask it what it remembers, and add or remove memories.

✓

Firms: one question reaches the whole team

In a Professional Organization twin, an administrator's question can draw on the team's twins — ask "Where do the Q3 installs stand?" and the answer comes back without chasing anyone.

Generate reports

Reports are template-driven and two-phase, so output matches the format you already use and nothing is generated until you approve it.

1

Template lookup

Mente searches your sources for a matching template. If it can't find one, it asks you to upload an example rather than inventing a format.

2

Plan — you approve

Mente shows a Report Plan: the template it will follow, the data source, the scope, the output format, and an ETA. Nothing happens until you say go.

3

Generate

It produces a PDF, Excel, or Word file matching the template and saves it to the Reports tab. Frequent reports can be saved as Tasks and re-run later.

Administrators also get business-intelligence views: cross-team pattern discovery, operational event tracking, client/supplier relationship intelligence, and leadership-facing summaries individual employees don't see.

If you have a Legal Advisor twin

Legal twins run a legal-specialized Mente — built for legal knowledge work, with citations and reasoning audits.

CapabilityWhat it does
Case-event trackingFilings, hearings, rulings, and deadlines on a timeline, with procedural-risk surfacing.
Judge & court profilingHow a chamber treats a doctrine; how a judge rules on a motion type.
Reasoning-chain auditTraces rulings to judges, doctrine to norms, opinions to their source rulings.
Drafting with citationsBriefs, opinion drafts, case summaries, judge/court profiles — each citing article/section, court/chamber, and date.
Note for legal twins: the generic Reports tab is hidden. Legal output — briefs, opinions, case summaries — still generates through chat; the standard reports surface is simply suppressed.

Languages

ProAsiste serves Spanish (LATAM) and English, with support for Portuguese, French, and German. Your default follows your region; the brand names Mente and ProAsiste stay the same in every language — only the explanatory text is localized.
