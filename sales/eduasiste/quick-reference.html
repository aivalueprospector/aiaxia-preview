EduAsiste Sales Quick Reference Card

Print this single page for quick reference during demos and calls.

30-Second Elevator Pitch

"EduAsiste gives every learner Eduardo, a patient AI study buddy that guides with questions instead of just handing over answers. Parents, tutors, and teachers get a dashboard that stays in sync, so you always know how your child is growing, and you can guide them without nagging. Ask Eduardo a question, get a real answer pulled straight from their work. Assign practice in one sentence. It's like having a teacher who reports to you after every lesson, plus a tutor who never loses patience."

The Tabs (Right Panel)

Three tabs, framed for learning.

Tab One-Liner Key Demo Action
Notes "See what your child has learned and where they're stuck" Open Notes to show the concepts captured from recent study sessions
Uploads "Drop a worksheet or reading, it becomes practice" Drag-drop a PDF reading and watch it turn into study material
Assignments "Assign in one sentence" Show an assignment: one line in, practice + answer key out

Pain Points → Solutions

They Say You Say
"I never know what my child actually learned today" "Just ask Eduardo. Live answer, pulled straight from their work. No waiting for report cards."
"Building practice and worksheets takes all evening" "Type one sentence. You get the worksheet for them and a private answer key for you."
"My kid just wants the answer, not to learn it" "Eduardo guides Socratically. It asks questions instead of handing over answers, so real understanding sticks."
"I want quality learning time but I'm not the tutor" "Eduardo runs the session and summarizes what clicked and what didn't, right in your dashboard."
"Goals get set and then forgotten" "Set a goal like 'read 20 books this semester' and the bar fills in automatically as they progress."
"I worry about privacy and surveillance" "Full visibility for you, privacy for your child. You're the account owner and you read everything; their work never trains public AI models."

Quick ROI Math

Time Saved Building Practice:

  • 45 min/night × 4 nights × 36 school weeks = ~108 hours/year
  • That's three full weeks of evenings handed back to the family

Tutoring Coverage:

  • A private tutor runs 40−80/hour; even one session a week is 1, 400−2,800/year
  • Eduardo guides between sessions, every day, for a fraction of that

Learning Retention:

  • Socratic guidance builds understanding that survives the test
  • The concept map captures it continuously, so nothing has to be re-taught from scratch

Objection Killers

Objection One-Line Response
"We already use a homework app / learning site" "Those deliver content. EduAsiste understands YOUR child and reports back to you."
"Is my child's data safe?" "Dedicated environment. Their data never trains public models, and you control who sees what."
"Won't they just let the AI do the work?" "It asks questions instead of giving answers. It's built to make them think, not copy."
"How is this different from ChatGPT?" "ChatGPT knows the internet. EduAsiste knows YOUR learner, and keeps you in the loop."
"No time to set it up" "30 minutes. Then it learns from normal study sessions on its own."

Demo Flow (15 minutes)

  1. Chat (3 min): Ask "How's Emma doing in reading this week?" and show the live answer
  2. Notes (3 min): Show what the learner has learned and where they're stuck
  3. Assignments (3 min): Assign practice in one sentence, then reveal the private answer key
  4. Uploads (3 min): Drop a reading and watch it become study material
  5. Close (3 min): Recap the value and the fit for the family or school
