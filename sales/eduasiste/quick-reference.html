EduAsiste Sales Quick Reference Card

Print this single page for quick reference during demos and calls.

30-Second Elevator Pitch

"EduAsiste gives every learner their own AI twin — a patient tutor that guides with questions instead of just handing over answers. Parents, tutors, and teachers get a twin too, and the twins talk to each other. So you always know how your child is growing, and you can guide them without nagging. Ask a question, get a real answer pulled straight from their learning. Assign practice in one sentence. It's like having a teacher who reports to you after every lesson, and a tutor who never loses patience."

Pain Points → Solutions

They Say You Say
"I never know what my child actually learned today" "Just ask your twin. Live answer, pulled straight from their learning. No waiting for report cards."
"Building practice and worksheets takes all evening" "Type one sentence. You get the worksheet for them and a private answer key for you."
"My kid just wants the answer, not to learn it" "Their twin guides Socratically — it asks questions instead of handing over answers. Real understanding sticks."
"I want quality learning time but I'm not the tutor" "Their twin runs the session and summarizes what clicked and what didn't, right in your chat."
"Goals get set and then forgotten" "Set a goal like 'read 20 books this semester' and the bar fills in automatically as they progress."
"I worry about privacy and surveillance" "Full transparency, built in. Every time you check their data, they can see it happened. That's how it works by default."

Quick ROI Math

Time Saved Building Practice:

  • 45 min/night × 4 nights × 36 school weeks = ~108 hours/year
  • That's three full weeks of evenings handed back to the family

Tutoring Coverage:

  • A private tutor runs 40−80/hour; even one session a week is 1, 400−2,800/year
  • A learner twin guides between sessions, every day, for the price of a tier

Learning Retention:

  • Socratic guidance builds understanding that survives the test
  • The concept map captures it continuously, so nothing has to be re-taught from scratch

Objection Killers

Objection One-Line Response
"We already use a homework app / learning site" "Those deliver content. EduAsiste understands YOUR child and reports back to you."
"Is my child's data safe?" "Dedicated environment. Their data never trains public models, and every access is visible to them."
"Won't they just let the AI do the work?" "It asks questions instead of giving answers. It's built to make them think, not copy."
"How is this different from ChatGPT?" "ChatGPT knows the internet. EduAsiste knows YOUR learner, and keeps you in the loop."
"No time to set it up" "30 minutes. Then it learns from normal study sessions on its own."

Demo Flow (15 minutes)

  1. Chat (3 min) — Ask "How's Emma doing in reading this week?" and show the live answer
  2. DATA (3 min) — Show how a learner's concepts connect on the graph
  3. TASKS (3 min) — Assign practice in one sentence; reveal the private answer key
  4. DOCS (3 min) — Drop a reading and watch it become study material
  5. Close (3 min) — Recap the value, discuss which tier fits the family or school
