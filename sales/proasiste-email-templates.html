Email Best Practices

Subject Line Rules

  • Keep under 50 characters
  • Make it specific to them
  • Avoid spam triggers (FREE, LIMITED TIME, ACT NOW)
  • Questions often outperform statements

Body Rules

  • Keep first email under 150 words
  • One clear call to action
  • Make it easy to say yes or no
  • Personalize with company name, industry, or recent news

Timing

  • Tuesday-Thursday typically best
  • 8-10 AM or 2-4 PM local time
  • Avoid Mondays (inbox overload) and Fridays (weekend mode)

Follow-Up Cadence

  • Day 1: Initial email
  • Day 3: Follow-up if no response
  • Day 7: Different angle/value prop
  • Day 14: Final attempt
  • Then: Move to nurture sequence
