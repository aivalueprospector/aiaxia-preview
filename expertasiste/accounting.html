Built for client confidentiality

Your client financial data is not a training dataset. It stays yours.

Client data isolation

Each client's documents live in their own isolated workspace. A search in Client A's context cannot surface Client B's files.

End-to-end encryption

Common questions

From contadores who pushed back hard before they signed up.

Does ExpertAsiste replace my tax software?
No. ExpertAsiste is a knowledge management and communication tool — it doesn't calculate taxes, file returns, or replace your compliance stack. It handles the research, memo-writing, and client communication layer that surrounds your core compliance work.
What regulations can it look up?
The regulations it searches are the ones you upload. If you've curated the SII circulares relevant to your practice, it searches those. We don't inject external regulatory content we haven't verified with you — that's an accuracy risk for your professional advice.
How does it handle regulation changes mid-year?
You upload the updated circular or bulletin. Mente indexes it and flags any conflicts with prior interpretations in your knowledge base. You decide how to reconcile the change — and ExpertAsiste helps you draft the client memo explaining what changed.
Can my junior associates use it?
Yes. Role-based access lets you control which clients each associate can see. Goal: team-level workspaces with shared knowledge bases and separate per-associate activity logs in Q4 2026.
