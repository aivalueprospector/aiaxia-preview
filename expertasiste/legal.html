Common questions

From abogados who asked us the hard questions.

Does ExpertAsiste generate legal advice I can send directly to clients?
No. ExpertAsiste generates drafts grounded in your prior work — which you review, edit, and sign off before anything goes to a client. The advice is yours. Mente is your research and drafting assistant, not your co-counsel.
Who can see my uploaded case files?
Only you (and team members you explicitly grant access). Your workspace is isolated — no other ExpertAsiste user can search your files. We do not use your matter data to train AI models.
What jurisdictions does the case precedent search cover?
Precedent search works on documents you upload. If you've handled matters in Chilean commercial law, your search covers Chilean commercial law as documented in your files. We don't inject external case law you haven't validated — that's a accuracy and privilege risk we won't take.
Can I use this with my existing billing system?
Goal: integration with common billing systems by end of 2026. In the meantime, ExpertAsiste tracks time-on-matter for every AI-assisted action so you have a log to reference when billing.
