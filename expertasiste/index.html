How ExpertAsiste works

Three steps from expertise to engaged client.

1

Build your expert profile

Describe your specialty, years of experience, and the types of matters you take. Mente learns from your uploaded documents to surface your knowledge on demand.

2

Accept consultancy requests

Clients find you by vertical and expertise. AI-powered intake screens requests and surfaces relevant context before your first conversation.

3

Deliver AI-assisted advice

Search your prior work, generate client-ready memos, and track deadlines — all from one workspace purpose-built for subject-matter experts.
