Our mission

What we believe

You own your data

Export anytime. Obsidian vault, Google Drive, Neo4j dump. If you leave, take everything. No lock-in, no hostage data.

Structure beats memory

Conversations fade. A knowledge graph grows. Every interaction adds nodes and relationships that compound over time.

AI should do work, not just talk

Chat is the starting point. Tasks, document mining, reports, calendar sync — your twin does real work with real outputs.

Human in the loop

Nothing hits your graph without your approval. Verify-AI lets you review, edit, and reject before anything is committed.
