Questions service businesses ask

We already use a field service app. Do we need to replace it?
No. Mente ingests files you export from your existing tools — CSVs, PDFs, spreadsheets. Most teams run Mente alongside their dispatch or scheduling app, using Mente for search, reporting, and cross-job intelligence. Integration and sync options are on the roadmap.
Our records are a mess — some on paper, some in email, some in a spreadsheet nobody fully understands. Can Mente handle that?
That's exactly what Mente is designed for. You upload whatever you have — scanned paper forms, email exports, spreadsheets, PDFs — and Mente normalizes it all into a searchable knowledge base. You don't need clean data to start getting value.
Can technicians access it from the field?
Yes. ProAsiste runs in any browser. Techs can pull up customer history, check the last service notes, or look up a part spec from their phone before knocking on the door.
How many seats do I need?
ProAsiste is priced at $79/seat/month, up to 5 seats. Most service shops run 1–3 seats: one for the owner, one for office staff, and optionally one shared among field techs. Tell us about your setup and we'll recommend what fits.
How long before it's actually useful?
Most businesses see value within a few days of the first document upload. Mente starts indexing immediately, and useful answers appear as soon as a few dozen records are in the system.
