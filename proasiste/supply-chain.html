Questions supply chain teams ask

We have documents spread across an ERP, email, and a shared drive. Can Mente handle that?
Yes. Mente ingests whatever you can export — PDFs, XLSX, CSV, DOCX, and scanned documents. Most teams start by exporting from their ERP and uploading alongside email attachments and drive files. Native ERP integration is on the roadmap.
How does Mente catch price discrepancies — does it need structured data?
No. Mente reads unstructured documents — a PDF invoice and a PDF purchase order — extracts the relevant fields, and compares them. You don't need to pre-process or structure your documents to get reconciliation results.
We have hundreds of suppliers and thousands of documents. Can it scale?
ProAsiste is built for document-heavy businesses. There's no hard cap on ingestion volume. For businesses with very large document sets, reach out and we'll discuss what fits your operation and volume.
Can Mente produce reports I can send to my finance team or auditors?
Yes. Mente generates formatted exports as PDF, XLSX, or DOCX. Every claim in a Mente report is grounded in your source documents, with citations — which makes them useful for audit purposes.
How many seats do we need?
ProAsiste is priced at $79/seat/month, up to 5 seats. Supply chain teams typically need 2–4 seats: procurement, operations, and finance. For teams larger than 5, reach out and we'll talk options.
