Questions parents ask

What ages is Eduardo designed for?
Ages 8 to 18. Eduardo adapts tone, vocabulary, and content filters to the child's grade level — a 3rd-grader sees short, encouraging prompts; a high-schooler sees deeper Socratic probing. Under 13, content moderation is stricter and COPPA parental-consent rules apply.
Is this safe for my kid?
COPPA-compliant. End-to-end encrypted. You read every conversation. We never sell data. You control access - you can pause or delete everything at any time. No PIN or separate login for the child — the parent stays the account owner and monitors full history.
Can my child chat with Eduardo outside of homework?
Yes. Children can start free-form conversations with Eduardo — curiosity questions, stories, "how do airplanes work?" Eduardo stays on the Socratic path (questions, not answers) and you see every exchange in your parent dashboard.
Won't my child just ask Eduardo for the answer?
Eduardo is built on the Socratic method and explicitly refuses to give answers. Your child has to work through every problem. You can see every attempt and every nudge Eduardo gave.
Does this replace a human tutor?
No. It makes your time - or a tutor's time - 10× more effective between sessions. A tutor meets your kid once a week. Eduardo is there every night.
How does onboarding work?
You set up the child's profile during signup — name, age, grade, state for curriculum alignment, subjects they're working on. Each child gets their own private workspace and document folder, separate from yours but fully visible to you. No setup required from the child — they sign in and start learning.
What if my kid hates it?
First week is free. Cancel anytime, no questions. If Eduardo isn't earning its keep in 30 days, you get a refund.
